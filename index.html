STAGES REHEARSAL PRICING

Minimum of 60$ just to open the door. You can stay up to 4 hours at that rate.

Anything after that is $10 an hour extra.

If you want to do 1 day a week and pay in advance for the month, it will be $200, which works out to be $50 per session (4 4 hour sessions per month).

If you have drum or bass we can do weekends anytime, but weekdays only after 3pm for noise ordinance. Quieter acoustic practice/shows are fine anytime.
